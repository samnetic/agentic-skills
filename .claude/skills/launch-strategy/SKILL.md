# Launch Strategy

Plan launches as multi-phase systems, not one-day events.

## Workflow

1. Define launch scope, goals, and success metrics.
2. Segment audience and choose channel mix.
3. Build phased execution plan (prelaunch, launch, postlaunch).
4. Prepare assets, sales/support enablement, and operations.
5. Execute with real-time monitoring.
6. Run post-launch review and iteration.

## Required Inputs

- Launch type (new product, feature, reposition, market expansion)
- Target segments and key message
- Available channels and team owners
- Commercial goals and measurement windows

## Execution Protocol

### 1) Prelaunch

- Clarify positioning and offer
- Finalize asset plan and owners
- Validate operational readiness

### 2) Launch Day

- Activate channels in planned sequence
- Monitor leading indicators and feedback
- Respond to objections quickly

### 3) Postlaunch

- Analyze results against goals
- Capture learnings and next campaign actions
- Feed insights to product and growth backlog

## Output Contract

Deliver:

1. Launch plan by phase with owners
2. Asset matrix by channel and audience
3. Post-launch review with decisions

## Quality Gates

- Success metrics and thresholds are explicit.
- Every launch activity has owner and deadline.
- Sales/support are enabled before launch day.
- Post-launch review is completed within 7 days.

## Anti-Patterns

- Announcing without activation and retention follow-through.
- Treating channel posting as launch strategy.
- No feedback loop from launch outcomes to roadmap.
