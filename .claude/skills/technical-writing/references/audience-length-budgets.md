# Audience and Length Budgets

| Mode | Tone | Required Signals | Max Words |
|---|---|---|---:|
| `exec` | Strategic, concise | Decision, impact, risk, ask | 500 |
| `product` | User and outcome focused | Problem, scope, metrics, trade-offs | 1100 |
| `engineering` | Operational and precise | Inputs, steps, edge cases, checks | 1400 |
| `reference` | Neutral and exact | Definitions, options, examples | 1800 |

If the user requests depth beyond the budget, keep core content in budget and
move details to appendices.
