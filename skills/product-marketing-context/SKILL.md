# Product Marketing Context

Maintain one canonical source of truth for market and messaging assumptions.

## Workflow

1. Collect current product facts and constraints.
2. Define ICP and non-ICP segments.
3. Document core problem, alternatives, and differentiation.
4. Capture pricing motion, GTM channels, and proof assets.
5. Publish and version a single context file.
6. Review and refresh on a fixed cadence.

## Required Inputs

- Product capabilities and roadmap constraints
- Best-fit customers and buying process
- Competitive alternatives and positioning stance
- Current GTM, pricing, and activation motion

## Progressive Disclosure Map

- Primary artifact template: [references/context-template.md](references/context-template.md)
- Update checklist: [references/context-update-checklist.md](references/context-update-checklist.md)

## Execution Protocol

Create or update `.agents/product-marketing-context.md` using the template.

Include these sections:

- Product summary and target outcomes
- ICP and non-ICP
- Problem framing and alternatives
- Differentiation and proof points
- Pricing and packaging snapshot
- Channel strategy and current priorities
- Open risks and unknowns

## Output Contract

Deliver:

1. Updated `.agents/product-marketing-context.md`
2. Change log note (what changed and why)
3. Next review date and owner

## Quality Gates

- ICP/non-ICP boundaries are explicit.
- Claims are backed by evidence or labeled assumptions.
- Pricing/GTM sections reflect current operating reality.
- Last-updated date is included.

## Anti-Patterns

- Storing duplicate conflicting context in multiple files.
- Mixing aspirational messaging with current truth.
- Omitting non-ICP boundaries.
